Decentralized Autonomous organizations are collectively owned organizations that are governed by smart contracts with no central authority. The members  hold tokens that grant them voting rights and rights to participate in governance activities such as technical upgrades and treasury allocation

[ERC-1202: Voting Interface](https://eips.ethereum.org/EIPS/eip-1202)

[ERC-1261: Membership Verification Token (MVT)](https://eips.ethereum.org/EIPS/eip-1261)

[ERC-1417: Poll Standard](https://eips.ethereum.org/EIPS/eip-1417)

[ERC-3000: Optimistic enactment governance standard](https://eips.ethereum.org/EIPS/eip-3000)

[ERC-4824: Common Interfaces for DAOs](https://eips.ethereum.org/EIPS/eip-4824)
